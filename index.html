About

Hi! I'm Alex. I search out and squash software bugs.

I've been writing code ever since I turned eleven and an owl didn't deliver my Hogwarts letter. Magic never lost its appeal, and aside from Universal Studios, creating for the web is about as close as the wizarding world as I can imagine.

Before I made web applications, I spent a few years in fashion PR. Most of my work was on the digital side (online marketing, eCommerce, etc.), but I've also planned some great events and directed a photoshoot or two.

Things I do in my spare time include photography, programming Slack bots and yoga.
