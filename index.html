About Alex

Before I made web applications, I spent a few years in fashion PR. Most of my work was on the digital side (online marketing, eCommerce, etc.), but I've also planned some great events and directed a photoshoot or two.

I graduated from Lehigh University in 2009 and General Assembly's Web Development Immersive in 2014.

I've been writing code ever since the summer after my 11th birthday passed and I didn't receive my Hogwarts letter. Magic never lost its appeal, and creating things for the web is about as close to Hogwarts as I'm going to get.

Some other things I like are coffee, pad kee mao, vintage jewelry, photography, travel and yoga.
